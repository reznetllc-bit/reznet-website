What RezNet Does During a Wi-Fi Optimization

This isn’t “turn it off and on again.” We treat your home like a mini enterprise network and give you real data and real improvements.

1

Walk-through & Interview

We walk the home with you, hear the pain points, and note the devices and routines that actually matter.

2

Live Signal Survey

We use professional survey tools to create heatmaps of your real Wi-Fi coverage and signal quality.

3

Channel & Power Tuning

We adjust channels, power levels, and roaming behavior to reduce interference and smooth out coverage.

4

Placement & Backhaul Fixes

We recommend (and optionally execute) smarter placement, wiring a mesh node, or relocating hardware.

5

RezNet Certified Report

You receive a clear report with before/after heatmaps, findings, and suggestions for future upgrades.
