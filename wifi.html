What RezNet Does During a Wi-Fi Optimization

This isn’t “turn it off and on again.” We treat your home like a mini enterprise network.

1

Walk-Through & Interview

We walk the house with you, listen to what’s not working, and note devices, rooms, and routines that matter most.

2

Live Signal Survey

Using professional survey tools, we measure signal and create heatmaps so you can see exactly where Wi-Fi is strong or weak.

3

Channel & Power Tuning

We adjust channels, transmit power, and band steering to reduce interference and smooth out coverage.

4

Placement & Backhaul Fixes

We recommend smarter placement for routers/mesh nodes and, when possible, wire key nodes with Ethernet for real backhaul.

5

RezNet Certified Report

You get a clear before/after report with heatmaps, findings, and next-step recommendations. Proof, not promises.
