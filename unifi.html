Is UniFi Right For Me?

UniFi installations are ideal when you need more than a quick tune-up.

Large or Multi-Story Home

Your home’s size or layout means one router will never cover it reliably.

Device-Heavy Household

You’ve got dozens of devices – phones, TVs, consoles, smart home gear – all online at once.

Work From Home / Business

You rely on rock-solid Wi-Fi for calls, remote work, or running a home office.

New Build or Renovation

You’re building or remodeling and want to bake wired coverage into the walls from day one.

How a UniFi Install Works

We treat your home the way a business treats a critical office network – but with more respect for the drywall.

1

Consultation & Design

We review your layout, needs, and construction, then design a UniFi layout: how many APs, where they go, and how they connect.

2

Professional Installation

We run Ethernet where needed, mount access points cleanly on ceilings or walls, and set up the UniFi controller and switching.

3

Configuration & Tuning

We configure SSIDs, channels, power levels, guest networks, and security so your network is fast, stable, and safe.

4

Testing & Handover

We test coverage and speeds, generate a RezNet Certified report, and show you how to use your new system (or manage it for you).
