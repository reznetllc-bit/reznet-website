Why RezNet

RezNet exists because homeowners deserve better Wi-Fi and clearer answers. We’re not here to push boxes – we’re here to design, educate, and stand behind the networks we build.

Our Core Principles

Education First

We explain every recommendation in plain language. When we leave, you understand what changed and why your network behaves better.

Optimize Before Replace

We tune and test what you have before suggesting new hardware. If we recommend upgrades, you’ll know it’s for a good reason.

Private by Default

Your network and cameras can run entirely on hardware in your home. We prefer local-first solutions and don’t push unnecessary cloud services.

Verified Results

We measure and document everything. You get heatmaps, test results, and a RezNet Certified report instead of “trust us, it’s better.”

About the Company

RezNet LLC is a local, owner-operated company based in Davie County, North Carolina. We focus on one thing: building and maintaining home networks that feel invisible – they just work.

We bring enterprise-style engineering down to earth for homeowners. That means using professional tools, proven best practices, and clear communication instead of jargon.

Local, On Purpose
