Why RezNet exists

Most residential networks are installed — not evaluated. ISPs sell speed. Retailers sell routers. Homeowners are left with speed tests and guesses.

RezNet’s job is to make the network measurable, explainable, and fixable — in plain language — so decisions are obvious and defensible.

Evidence

Coverage, stability, interference, wired readiness, and bottleneck diagnosis.

Verdict

Clear pass/fail logic, risk flags, constraints, and what not to buy.

Roadmap

Phased upgrades: immediate wins → future-proofing → optional implementation spec.

Primary clients

Realtors. Buyers. Sellers.
