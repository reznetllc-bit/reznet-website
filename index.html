How RezNet Works

A simple, repeatable process that turns “my Wi-Fi sucks” into “wow, this just works.”

1

Learn Your Home

We start with a conversation about what’s going wrong, what’s important, and what “good” looks like for your home.

2

Map & Model

We build a predictive Wi-Fi model, marking walls, materials, and access-point options so you can see coverage before hardware moves.

3

Survey & Diagnose

We walk your home with professional tools to measure real signal levels, noise, and interference.

4

Engineer & Optimize

We tune channels, power, placement, wiring, and configuration – whether you keep existing hardware or upgrade to UniFi.

5

Validate & Certify

You receive a RezNet Certified Network report with heatmaps, findings, and clear recommendations going forward.
